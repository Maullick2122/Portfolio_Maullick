ABOUT

I am Maullick Pushkarna, I would like to describe myself as a 3D generalist and concept artist with an inclination towards texturing, lighting, assembling and cinematics of an animation. Some of the projects that I have done in my time at university are under the projects page.


I have also developed work for VR headsets, mainly the Meta Quest 2 i.e project for the SDGs and also worked on the unconventional convention which entailed team building exercises through the use of immersive technologies. I currently work a part time at Coventry University as a student proctor where I work in immersive. This includes being technically adept with VR, AR and XR development softwares and also, using MO-CAP rigs like Shogun by Vicon.


Some of my references you can contact are Dr. Darren Berkland (They/Them) at ad5836@coventry.ac.uk and Dr. Bianca Wright (She/Her) at ab6597@coventry.ac.uk.
